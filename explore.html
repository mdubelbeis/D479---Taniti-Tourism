Explore Taniti

Taniti is a vibrant island with a variety of landscapes and attractions to explore:

  • Taniti City: Flat, walkable city with native architecture, shops, beaches, and public transit.
  • Yellow Leaf Bay: Popular area for tourists featuring sandy beaches and cruise ship access.
  • Merriton Landing: Rapidly developing entertainment district on the north side of the island.
  • Rainforest & Volcano: Explore the mountainous interior, including an active volcano.
  • Interactive Tours: Take part in boat or bus tours, helicopter rides, and zip-lining experiences.
