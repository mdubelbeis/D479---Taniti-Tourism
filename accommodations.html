Accommodations

Choose from a range of comfortable and safe lodging options:

  • Hostel: Budget-friendly option for backpackers and solo travelers.
  • Family-Owned Hotels: Personalized hospitality with authentic local charm.
  • Bed and Breakfasts: Ideal for couples seeking a peaceful stay.
  • Four-Star Resort: Luxurious full-service resort with beachfront access and modern amenities.

All accommodations are government-regulated and regularly inspected.
