Dining

Taniti offers diverse dining experiences:

  • Local Cuisine: 5 restaurants serve traditional island dishes, primarily fresh fish and rice.
  • American-style: 3 restaurants offer burgers, BBQ, and classic American fare.
  • Pan-Asian: 2 restaurants serve Asian fusion including sushi and Thai dishes.
  • Late Night Options: Note: Alcohol is not served between midnight and 9:00 a.m.
